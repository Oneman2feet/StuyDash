What is RestRate?

This app is designed to enable people without smartphones to increase the functionality of their phones through sms messaging. In order to use it they text the name of a restaurant and a zipcode or adress to the number 1-917-525-2351. The app will then return a text message with a rating for the restaurant based on the factual database. It will also return the sanitation rating given to every restaurant in the New York City Metropolitan area.

How to use RestRate

To use this app text 1-917-525-2351 a text in the format:restaurant name, zipcode, blah, blah. Then, after a few moments you will recive the restaurants rating. Then when you come to this site you can type your phone number, with no spaces and no 1 in the beginning, in the upper right corner and you will be able to see all the restraunts you have gotten reviews for.
