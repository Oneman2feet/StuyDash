Some Useful Disaster Tips
    1. On The Hurricane

A Hurricane is a large storm comprised of clouds, thunder, lightning, wind, and rain. It typically has an eye, but it is blind. Calling yourself 'Nobody' will not save you from a Hurricane however, as they tend to disregard this sort of thing. Hurricanes are born when the spirits of angry drowned marching-band brass players get together and decide to ruin everyone else's parade. Typically, Apocalyptic Hurricanes occur when the spirits of rival marching bands run into each other and try to outdoo the other's Hurricane which really just reasults in one big mess much to the distress of those still living.

    To save yourself:
    DON'T GO OUTSIDE. Seriously, you might get knocked off by all manner of objects caught in the wind: branches, bricks, turkeys. And turkeys are not a nice way to go.
    Stock up on foodstuffs. In the days after the hurricane has died down, you can use the food as leverage to get valuable commodities such as a year-long subscription to WoW.
    Keep cleaning supplies handy. You never know when the Johnsons might visit.
    In case your house/hovel/cardboard box is destroyed by the Hurricane, immediately find another shelter. No, umbrellas do not count. Unless Rihanna is holding one, in which case you're probably inside a mansion somewhere anyways.
